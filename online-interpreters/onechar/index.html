Use characters to control the asc value

  • +: Increment by 1
  • -: Decrement by 1
  • *: Square "asc"
  • /: Reset to 1
  • ^: Square "asc" then decrement "asc"
  • .: Append character to output
  • !: Debug
  • ;: Multiply by 2
